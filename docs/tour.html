  • 5-Day NYC Tour
    • Day 1

      Welcome to the Big Apple

      • Morning: Arrival at JFK International Airport. Transfer to the hotel and check-in.
      • Afternoon: Arrival at JFK International Airport. Transfer to the hotel and check-in.
      • Evening: Welcome dinner at a local hotspot, introducing you to the diverse culinary scene.
    • Day 2

      Manhattan Marvels

      • Morning: Visit Central Park for a leisurely stroll and perhaps a boat ride on the lake.
      • Afternoon: Discover the Metropolitan Museum of Art (Met) and its vast art collections.
      • Evening: Optional Broadway show or free time to explore th e city's nightlife.
    • Day 3

      Downtown Delights

      • Morning: Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island tour, immersing in American history.
      • Afternoon: Visit the iconic Grand Central Terminal, immersing yourself in its architectural splendor and historical significance.
      • Evening: Dinner in the trendy neighborhoods of SoHo or Tribeca.
    • Day 4

      Culture and Cuisine

      • Morning: Visit the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) to experience contemporary art.
      • Afternoon: Arrival at JFK International Airport. Transfer to the hotel and check-in.
      • Evening: Arrival at JFK International Airport. Transfer to the hotel and check-in.
    • Day 5

      Skyline and Farewell

      • Morning: Ascend to the Top of the Rock for breathtaking views of the city.
      • Afternoon: Explore the High Line, an elevated park with unique greenery and art installations.
      • Evening: Farewell dinner cruise, sailing along the Hudson River with views of the city lights.
