Tour FAQs

Answers to Your Essential Questions

  • How do I book the tour?
    To secure your spot, simply contact our booking department at info@adv.com or contact us
  • What is included in the tour package?
    The tour includes airport transfers, accommodation, daily breakfasts, a guided city tour, and excursions to iconic sites: the Metropolitan Museum of Art, Grand Central Terminal, Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island, 9/11 Memorial & Museum, and the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)
  • Are meals included in the tour price?
    While daily breakfasts are included, other meals are not part of the package to allow flexibility for personal dining preferences
  • Is travel insurance required?
    We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances, including trip cancellations, interruptions, or medical emergencies
  • Can I make changes to my reservation?
    Changes to the reservation, such as altering tour dates or the number of participants, may be subject to additional fees
  • Are there any optional activities or excursions available?
    Yes, there may be optional activities or excursions. Details and costs will be provided in advance, and you can decide whether to participate based on your preferences
